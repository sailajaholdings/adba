Our Achievements

ID: achievement-1

Rising Stars: Remarkable Play at the AP 10th Sub-Junior Basketball Finals 2025

The 10th AP Sub-Junior Inter District Basketball Championship, held at PES Public School grounds, Chittoor (AP) from May 15th-18th, 2025, saw our boys deliver an outstanding campaign. In a thrilling final, they narrowly fell to East Godavari with a score of 35–38. Despite the close loss, their remarkable play and unwavering determination throughout the game earned them significant recognition and respect from everyone involved in the competition.

ID: achievement-2

2024 Community Basketball Camp

ADBA hosted a successful basketball camp at SSBN Stadium in Ananthapuramu, engaging over 100 young athletes. The initiative fostered community spirit, promoted basketball, and provided training under our esteemed coaches, strengthening local talent development.

ID: achievement-3

Top-Three Finish at 2024 U23 District Championship

Our U23 team excelled at the 2024 Inter District 3x3 Basketball Championship in Vijayawada, securing a top-three position. Their dynamic play and teamwork highlighted ADBA’s commitment to nurturing competitive talent across age groups.
